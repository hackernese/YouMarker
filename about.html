YouMarker

About

↪ YouMarker is an open-sourced project which helps you keeping track of your youtube videos, this can be useful if you are intending to study a 5-10 long hours course from youtube or watch from so many sources and each source has its own specific timestamp indicating some information.

↪ It can be a bit of a hassle to keep tracks of everything you have watched and not knowing what that video was or which section of it that holds the information that you wish to recall.
